# EmporiUm West Region Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Overview

This project was completed as the Week 12 Power BI Capstone for the Year Up United Data Analytics Training Academy.

The objective of this project was to analyze four years of sales data for EmporiUm, a virtual student bookstore that operates both physical retail locations and an online store. I was assigned the **West Region** and developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to provide business insights for regional leadership.

---

## Business Objective

The goal of this analysis was to help regional directors, territory managers, and store managers better understand sales performance by identifying:

- Sales trends over time
- Top-performing product categories
- Highest-performing stores
- Regional sales performance
- Best-selling books and authors
- Actionable business recommendations

**Region:** West

## Dashboard Pages

### Page 1 – West Region Sales Overview

This executive dashboard provides a high-level overview of sales performance for the West Region.

Features include:

- Total Sales KPI
- Total Stores KPI
- Product Categories KPI
- Total Transactions KPI
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Sales by Product Category
- Top Performing Stores

---

### Page 2 – West Region Sales Details

This page provides additional detail into regional sales performance.

Features include:

- Sales Distribution by State
- Category Sales Mix
- Top Selling Books
- Best-Selling Authors

---

### Page 3 – Key Insights & Recommendations

This page summarizes the analysis and provides recommendations for business decision makers.

Key findings include:

- Technology & Accessories generated approximately 72% of total regional revenue.
- Denver was the highest-performing store, generating over $917K in sales.
- The West Region generated approximately $6.4M in sales across 46K transactions.
- Colorado accounted for 100% of West Region sales.

Recommendations include:

- Expand Technology & Accessories inventory.
- Replicate Denver's successful sales strategies across other locations.
- Promote bestselling authors and popular book titles.
- Diversify revenue streams by investing in additional product categories.

- Creating DAX measures

## Key Business Insights

- Total West Region Sales: **$6.4M**
- Total Transactions: **46K**
- Total Stores: **18**
- Technology & Accessories represented approximately **72%** of total revenue.
- Denver was the top-performing store with over **$917K** in sales.
- Colorado represented all sales within the assigned West Region.
